---
title: "Instructor tools overview"
description: "Author exercises, monitor the live class dashboard, review per-student reasoning, and read cohort analytics — instructor tools that need no login."
---

Maieutic gives instructors three purpose-built views that surface learning signals a traditional lab cannot. You author exercises by describing them in plain text and letting Opus generate the specification scaffolding. During a session you watch a live dashboard that summarises, in plain language, where every active student is in their thinking. After a session you can open any student's full reasoning trail or read a cohort-level narrative of how the class engaged with the exercise as a whole.

No login is required. Navigate directly to `/live` to reach the dashboard, or use the instructor navigation to switch between tools.

<Note>
  Instructor pages are not behind an authentication gate. The landing page routes visitors based on whether they identify as a student or a teacher. Once on any instructor page you can navigate freely between `/live`, `/authoring`, `/cohorts`, and `/cohort/[id]` using the instructor navigation bar.
</Note>
